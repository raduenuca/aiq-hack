5. [Intro to Machine Learning (Udacity)](https://eu.udacity.com/course/intro-to-machine-learning--ud120)

9. [Artificial Intelligence Nanodegree (Udacity)](https://eu.udacity.com/course/ai-artificial-intelligence-nanodegree--nd898)
